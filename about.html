What is a Nuton ?

A nuton is a small mythical creature from the Belgian and French Ardennes, quite similar to a lutin. They used to live in caves or holes called "Trou de Nutons" in french that can be seen scattered across Wallonia. They are known to be great craftsmen, and used to repair shoes and cauldrons or sharpen knives of the people leaving their objects with a food offering at the entrance of their caves. Because the Nuton is also a little foodie !

I really enjoyed this picture of the maker, honing his skills and I want to add a more modern twist onto it, trying to create projects revolving around 3D printings or electronics.
